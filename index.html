Height Lines

Simple observations for your wellness.

An app that shows you the long-term and miniscule changes in your body so you know what is working for you and what is not.


Checkout link for early access.


How do I use it?
You simply talk to the app and use your phone's camera to show it the things you want to keep track of.
Is it only iOS?
Early access is only for the iOS app. You can register below for other platforms.
Is it free?
Not yet.
What is the tech behind?
It uses language models and computer vision to understand the camera input and communicate with you in natural language.
Does it work offline?
It is meant to be an offline app. One of the models is not yet quantized to run on metal so it uses cloud compute for that bit.

You can also just message me or register here if you're interested to use it in other platforms or get a binary executable.
